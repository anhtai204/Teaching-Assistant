# Exam Review Guide

Students preparing for the final exam should focus on the following topics:

1. Transformer architecture
2. Self-attention mechanism
3. Machine learning fundamentals
4. Differences between supervised and unsupervised learning

## Study Tips

- Review lecture slides weekly
- Practice explaining concepts in your own words
- Focus on understanding rather than memorization

## Important Topics

The most important topic for the exam is **Transformer architecture and attention mechanisms**.
